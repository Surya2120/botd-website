What Is BOTD

Where digital auditions meet live stage performance

Battle Of The Dance (BOTD) is a next-generation platform that connects digital auditions with live stage performances. It is designed to give dancers a fair chance to be seen, recognized, and celebrated.

01

Digital First Entry

Dancers submit audition videos online, making participation accessible from anywhere.

02

Curated Selection

Promising contestants are shortlisted to move from screens to the spotlight.

03

Live Performance Finish

Selected dancers perform live before judges and an engaged audience, creating a high-stakes finale.
