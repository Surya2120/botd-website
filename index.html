What Is BOTD

Where online auditions meet the energy of a live dance stage

Battle Of The Dance is built for dancers who want reach, visibility, and a real performance moment. It starts with video submissions and culminates in a live stage battle designed for audience excitement and talent discovery.

01

Digital First Entry

Dancers submit audition videos online, making participation accessible from anywhere.

02

Curated Selection

03

Live Performance Finish

Selected dancers perform live before judges and an engaged audience, creating a high-stakes finale.

Judges & Voting

Built for suspense, credibility, and crowd energy

BOTD combines expert evaluation with fan power. Three industry judges, one invisible judge, and audience participation create a format that feels both prestigious and unpredictable.

3 Industry Judges

Experienced dance professionals evaluate technique, stage command, and performance quality.

1 Invisible Judge

A hidden evaluation layer adds drama, fairness, and anticipation to the final results.
