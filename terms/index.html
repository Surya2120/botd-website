BOTD Participation Terms

Clear rules for registration, conduct, participation, and organizer rights.

Platform Purpose

BOTD is a dance competition platform created to discover, evaluate, and promote performers through structured auditions, events, and showcase opportunities.

User Responsibilities

  • Users must provide accurate registration details and submit original or authorized performance content.
  • Participants must follow BOTD rules, stage directions, timelines, and communication from the organizers.
  • Registration is required for participation in official BOTD selection rounds or event-related activities.

Organizer Rights

  • BOTD may review, shortlist, reject, or disqualify entries that are incomplete, misleading, duplicated, offensive, or rule-violating.
  • Schedules, rounds, event formats, and stage plans may change whenever operationally required.
  • BOTD may use approved participant names, images, audio, and video for judging, publishing, and promotional communication.

Entry Fee & Payment

  • Participants must pay the specified entry fee to complete registration for BOTD.
  • The entry fee is charged for participation in the competition and evaluation process.
  • Payments are processed securely through third-party payment gateways.

Refund Policy

  • All payments are subject to the BOTD Refund Policy.
  • Participants are advised to review the Refund Policy before making payment.

Eligibility

  • Participants must meet the eligibility criteria defined for BOTD events.
  • BOTD reserves the right to verify participant details and reject invalid entries.

Limitation of Liability

  • BOTD and Bee Infinity Groups are not responsible for any direct or indirect loss, damage, or injury arising from participation.
  • Participants are responsible for their own safety and compliance with applicable laws.
