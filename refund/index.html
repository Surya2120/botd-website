Refund Policy

Registration Payments Are Non-Refundable

This policy applies to BOTD registration-related payments once a participant proceeds successfully.

Payment Policy

  • All payments made for BOTD registrations are non-refundable.
  • Once a registration is completed successfully, cancellation is not allowed.
  • The registration fee is charged for participation in the Battle Of The Dance (BOTD) competition and evaluation process.
  • Payments are processed securely through third-party payment gateways.

Exceptions

  • Refunds will only be considered in case of duplicate payments or proven technical errors.
  • Approved refunds (if any) will be processed within 7–10 business days.

Event Changes

  • If BOTD modifies event schedules, rounds, or formats, the registration will remain valid.
  • No refunds will be issued due to operational or scheduling changes.

Policy Acceptance

  • By completing the payment, participants agree to this Refund Policy.
  • Participants are advised to review this policy before making any payment.
